Unlike our other subsystems, we can't just drop original phpBB files here.
This copy of phpBB consists of the following:

* phpBB3 sources
* Many files patched due to the latest phpBB-embed patch from the "phpbbforum" Drupal module project
* phpbbdrupalbridge includes from the "phpbbforum" Drupal module project
* de, es_x_tu, fr, it, ru language packs
* Additional "prosilver-embed" and "subsilver2-embed" styles
